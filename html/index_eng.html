Yhtä - Yhtenäisesti älykäs kaupunki

Yhtä is a project that explores how digital infrastructure of Tampere can be utilized.
YHTÄ-playbook is a result of this venture. This playbook enables any software developer using data produced by Tampere's ecosystem to build their own services.
Primary focus is on making Tampere a safe city for pedestrians.
Business Information Systems students from Tampere University of Applied Sciences are using this data in their games.
HANE uses data from Tampere in JSON format about local schools in their game Stop, Spot, Go! The game's map mirrors the city of Tampere, with added info-buttons to showcase which building or landmark is displayed and what the nearest school to that is. This adds more informative content to the game, as well as a genuine feel of Tampere's traffic environment.

STOP, SPOT, GO!

Welcome to an exciting and educational game on the streets of Tampere!

This is an addictive point-and-click puzzle, where you can test your focus and attention to details. Move from one level to another by solving puzzles and spotting dangers in traffic, all in the colorful city of Tampere!

In the game the player moves along in Tampere as a pedestrian. It's not as easy as it sounds! Each level has it's own problem: you will have to spot and solve these problems to move on. Be careful though, if you make too many mistakes, you will have to start over! This is fun game to learn about traffic safety and hone your problem solving skills!
